Individual Counseling

Call (310) 206-2875 for a 30-minute appointment with a counselor at the SRC

If you are coming for an individual counseling appointment:

  • Please bring a copy of the application(s) that you would like to discuss and any questions you have about it.

If you are coming for a writing support or proofreading appointment:

  • Call (310) 206-2875 during our business hours at least 2-3 days before you’d like to schedule your appointment. You will be assigned to a counselor and given his/her email address.
  • Email your scholarship essay draft, writing prompt, and information about the scholarship to your counselor no later than 11 a.m. the day of your appointment.
  • In your email, please specify if you need help with content-related issues or proofreading.
  • Because we can only handle a maximum of 5 double-spaced pages per writing appointment, please choose a 5-page section of your essay for the counselor to review if your essay is longer than 5 pages.
  • If you are a graduate student, please visit the Graduate Writing Center first: http://gsrc.ucla.edu/gwc/
  • Please note: We can assist UCLA students with essays related to scholarships and fellowships only.
