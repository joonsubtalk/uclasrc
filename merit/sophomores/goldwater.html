Scholarship Description

For sophomores and juniors planning graduate study in Mathematics, Engineering, and the Sciences Outstanding UCLA sophomores and juniors who intend to pursue careers in mathematics, the natural sciences, and engineering are invited to apply for the Goldwater Scholarship. The Goldwater Scholarship and Excellence in Education Foundation awards up to 300 merit-based educational scholarships to college sophomores and juniors throughout the United States.

Award

Each scholarship will cover eligible expenses for undergraduate tuition, fees, books, and room and board up to $7,500 annually. Sophomore level nominees will be eligible for two years of support and junior level nominees for one year of support.

Eligibility

In order to be considered for UCLA nomination you must
  1. Be a sophomore or junior pursuing a bachelor's degree on a full-time basis. "Sophomore" is defined as a student who plans two more years of full-time undergraduate study beginning September 2014. "Junior" is defined as a student who plans one more year of full-time undergraduate study beginning September 2014
  2. Be enrolled in an undergraduate field of study that will lead to a career in mathematics, the natural sciences, or engineering (please note: pre-med students are eligible only if planning to enter a joint MD/PhD program with the goal of a research rather than a clinical career)
  3. Have a college grade point average of at least 3.0
  4. Be a U.S. citizen, a permanent resident, or a U.S. national

For more information, go to http://www.ugeducation.ucla.edu/honors/goldwater.html
